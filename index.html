Conflict

Percy finds out that his mother may still be alive and is being held captive by Hades in the underworld. This sparks his enthusiasm to embark on the quest of finding Zeus' missing master bolt that he is prophecied to find. His picks Annabeth and Grover as his trusty companions. He also discovers that he is the son of Posiedon, the god of the sea. This means that he is a forbidden child and if he does not find the master bolt, the gods will start a war because they believe that as a forbidden child, Percy must have been the one who stole it from Zeus.

Along their journey, Percy, Grover and Annabeth fight many monsters and goons of Hades. These are all trials and tribulations to help prove the worthiness of the three teenagers. Throughout these conflicts and problems, they learn and grow to become a stronger and more mature team. They fight monsters such as the fury, Chimera, Procustes, and Cerberus.
